ABOUT US:

Mental health is important at every stage of our life, from childhood and adolescenes through adulthood. However due to stress and busy schedules, people have very little time to consider it as a priority. Our website Inner Pieces is catered towards individuals who are willing to improve their mental health and not worrying about traveling to visit a psychiatrist. Our website is a one stop destination where people can take appointments and have sessions online, buy stress relieving tools, books and prescribed drugs. They can also create a playlist of relaxing melodies that can help users to relax and put them to sleep. Users can also post and read feedback on psychiatrists.
